# tech-rebuild-2026

Why am I doing this?
I am profoundly awed by AI and Technology, and am enamoured by how harnessing it intelligently can enable you to achieve endless possiblities.
However, I have been painfully made aware that my technical (hard) skills and communication (soft) skills are severly subpar, and continuing down this path will lead me to fail terribly in my career and in life. Unending inaction will not be tolerated anymore. 
This project is me taking a stand and choosing to do what it takes to build my BRIGHT future in Tech from scratch, through the religious adoption of my curriculum that aim to give the best possible foundation for the right mindsets, habits, hard skills and soft skils that will enable me to reach my goal.

Pillairs to ensure this journey is most rewarding?
1. I WILL BUILD no matter how ugly. 
2. I WILL STRUGGLE first, before resorting for help
3. I WILL ADOPT a "regulated effort" mechanism where I only struggle without help for a set period
4. I WILL DOCUMENT my journey religiously to capture my learnings
5. I WILL FOCUS while eliminating external and internal distractions
